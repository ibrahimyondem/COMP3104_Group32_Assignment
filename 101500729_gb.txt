George Brown College is one of the major, and most famous, colleges of Toronto, ON, Canada.
It was founded 58 years ago, and as of today, it offers more than 170 full-time programs in art and design, 
business, community services, early childhood education, construction and engineering technologies, 
health sciences, hospitality and culinary arts, preparatory studies, 
as well as specialized programs and services for recent immigrants and international students.
